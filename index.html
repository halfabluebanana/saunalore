    April 2024 • The Sauna Winter

    The sauna mysteriously closed one day. Some said the heater was broken. Others said they were fixing the flooring. No one knew what happened. Nor why it closed so suddenly. Noone knew when it would open again, if ever.



      Yellow X on door

      CAUTION and CIUDADO warn

      The sauna is closed



      I ask the front desk

      When will the sauna open?

      “In a week”, they say



      If heater parts spoilt

      Then why undo the flooring

      Unless, oh. perhaps…?



      the parts are delayed

      sauna parts in high demand

      we just want to sweat



      Now thinking about

      The physics of sauna rocks

      Stones with entropy



      Gripping news of rocks

      Hot on edge of my seat, pray

      haste; may friends have heat



      I wanna sauna

      but its under maintenance

      vital, please hurry



      expert nowhere found,

      running out of steam sandclock:

      ticking tick tock tick



      I am so sorry

      Here I take sweat for granted

      Sending heat your way
